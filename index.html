Getting Started

What to Expect in Your First Session

Starting counseling can feel overwhelming. Here's what your journey with us looks like.

1

Initial Contact

Call or email us to schedule your first appointment. We'll discuss your needs and answer any questions you have about the process.

2

First Session

In our first meeting, we'll discuss your goals, concerns, and history. This is a safe space to share what brought you here. Sessions typically last 50-60 minutes.

3

Treatment Plan

Together, we'll develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your unique needs, incorporating the therapeutic approaches that will serve you best.

4

Ongoing Support

We'll meet regularly (typically weekly or bi-weekly) to work through challenges, build skills, and celebrate your progress along the way.

Therapeutic Approach

Evidence-Based, Faith-Informed

We utilize various therapeutic modalities independently tailored to each client's unique needs.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Identifying and reshaping thought patterns that impact emotions and behavior.

Faith-Based Therapy

Integrating spiritual principles and scripture into the therapeutic journey.

Brief Strategic Family Therapy

Targeted interventions to improve family interactions and dynamics.

Motivational Interviewing

Empowering clients to discover their own motivation for change.

Solution-Focused Brief Therapy

Focusing on strengths and solutions rather than problems and deficits.

Holistic Approach

Addressing the whole person ‚Äî mind, body, and spirit ‚Äî in the healing process.
